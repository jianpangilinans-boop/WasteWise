IMPORTANT April 15, 2026

New Waste Segregation Guidelines Effective May 1, 2026

The City Government of Cabanatuan announces the implementation of enhanced waste segregation guidelines starting May 1, 2026. All residents are required to separate waste into four categories: Biodegradable, Non-Biodegradable, Recyclable, and Special/Hazardous Waste.

Free waste bins will be distributed to all barangays. Educational sessions will be conducted from April 20-30, 2026. Penalties for non-compliance will be enforced starting June 1, 2026.

Collection Schedule Change for April 19, 2026

Due to the National Holiday on April 19 (Maundy Thursday), waste collection schedules will be adjusted:
